Key facts timeline

1948
The Nakba

The mass displacement of 750,000 Palestinians.

The Beginning

More than 700,000 Palestinians fled or were expelled, many settling in Gaza.

1967
Occupation

Israel captures Gaza in the Six-Day War.

Military Rule

This marked the start of the ongoing military occupation involving control of borders and airspace.

2007
The Blockade

Land, air, and sea blockade imposed.

De-development

The blockade restricted goods and people, leading to economic collapse well before 2023.

2023
Total War

Massive offensive following Oct 7.

Catastrophe

Displacement of nearly the entire population and destruction of most housing stock.

2024
ICJ Ruling

"Plausible" Genocide ruling.

Justice?

ICJ orders measures to prevent genocide and ensure aid delivery.
